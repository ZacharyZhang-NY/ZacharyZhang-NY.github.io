About PinOn

PinOn, Inc. is a computer vision company located in New York, USA. Its main business is the application of artificial intelligence (AI), computer vision (CV) and big data intelligence in manufacturing and related industries. The core team members of the company have many years of experience in scientific research and product implementation in key technical fields such as artificial intelligence and computer vision in China and the United States, and have a number of technical barriers.
